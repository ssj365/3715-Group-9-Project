Our Campuses

    St. John's
    The province's capital city, St. John's, is home to the St. John's campus which offers a full range of academic programs in a modern, urban setting.

    Grenfell
    Grenfell Campus, located in Corner Brook on the scenic west coast of the island, is a liberal arts and science campus specializing in environmental and fine arts programs.

    Marine Institute
    The Fisheries and Marine Institute is one of the most comprehensive centre for education, training, applied research and industrial support for the ocean industries. Located on the edge of the Atlantic Ocean, we are one of the most respected centres of marine learning and applied research in the world.
